## Gig Description

**MedPOS: The Ultimate Pharmacy Management & POS SaaS Solution**

Are you looking for a professional, cloud-based POS system to manage your medical store or pharmacy? Or do you need a powerful, multi-tenant SaaS foundation for your next big project? **MedPOS** is a battle-tested, feature-rich solution specifically tailored for the pharmaceutical industry, particularly optimized for the Pakistani market.

### 💊 Why Choose MedPOS?
Managing a pharmacy is complex. From tracking thousands of medicine expiries to ensuring cashiers don't make mistakes, you need a system that is fast, secure, and accurate. MedPOS solves these problems with a modern, high-performance interface.

### 🚀 Key Features included:
*   **⚡ Lightning-Fast POS Billing:** Bill customers in seconds. Full barcode scanner support and instant search.
*   **📦 Smart Inventory & Expiry Tracking:** Get automatic alerts before medicines expire (7, 30, or 90 days). Never lose money on expired stock again.
*   **📂 Pre-loaded DRAP Database:** Comes with **3,000+ pre-verified Pakistani medicines**. No more manual data entry!
*   **👥 Multi-User Roles & Permissions:** Dedicated dashboards for Owners, Pharmacists, and Cashiers.
*   **📑 Advanced Audit Logs:** Track every single action—who sold what, when, and for how much. Zero room for theft or errors.
*   **📊 Comprehensive Reporting:** Detailed Sales, Profit/Loss, Tax, and Shift reports. Export to PDF/CSV with one click.
*   **💳 Payment Integrations:** Support for Cash, Card, and **Safepay** (for local Pakistani payments).
*   **📧 Automated Notifications:** Email alerts via **Resend** for stock levels and system updates.
*   **📱 Cloud-Based:** Access your store’s data from your phone, tablet, or laptop—anywhere in the world.

### 🛠️ Technical Stack (Modern & Scalable):
*   **Frontend:** Next.js 14 (App Router), TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
*   **Backend/Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL with RLS for multi-tenant isolation)
*   **UI Components:** Shadcn UI, Base UI, Lucide Icons
*   **State Management:** Zustand & TanStack Query
*   **Monitoring:** Sentry integration for error tracking
